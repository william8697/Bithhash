Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Bithash

How does Bithash generate returns for investors?

Bithash generates returns through our industrial-scale Bitcoin mining operations. We operate mining facilities powered by renewable energy that generate Bitcoin rewards. These rewards are distributed to investors according to their chosen investment plan.

Is my Bitcoin safe with Bithash?

Yes, we prioritize security with multi-signature cold storage for the majority of funds, regular third-party security audits, and institutional-grade infrastructure. We never take custody of your Bitcoin without proper collateral for loans.

What are the risks involved?

Like all investments, there are risks including Bitcoin price volatility and changes in mining difficulty. However, our fixed return plans are backed by our mining operations, providing more stability than many crypto investments. We recommend diversifying your investments.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Withdrawals can be requested at any time through your dashboard. Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. There are no limits on withdrawals, though large amounts may require additional verification for security purposes.
