Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How are mining rewards calculated?

Mining rewards are calculated based on your share of our total hash power, the current Bitcoin network difficulty, and the BTC/USD exchange rate at the time of payout. We use a proprietary algorithm that accounts for pool fees, electricity costs, and maintenance to determine daily distributions.

What is the minimum investment?

The minimum investment varies by plan, starting at $500 for our Starter Plan. Institutional investors can contact our sales team for custom solutions with higher minimums and preferential rates.

How often are payouts made?

Payouts are made daily at 00:00 UTC. You can choose to automatically reinvest your earnings or withdraw them to your connected wallet. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours on business days.

What happens when the Bitcoin halving occurs?

Our mining operations are optimized to remain profitable through halving events. We continuously upgrade our hardware and negotiate preferential electricity rates to maintain margins. During halvings, we typically see increased BTC prices which offset the reduced block rewards.

Can I visit your mining facilities?

Yes, accredited investors can schedule tours of our facilities with proper notice. We conduct quarterly open houses and provide virtual tours for all investors through our dashboard.
