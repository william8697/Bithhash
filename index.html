Revenue Distribution

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services

How does Bithash generate returns for investors?
Bithash operates industrial-scale Bitcoin mining facilities. When you invest, you're purchasing real hash power in our operations. We handle all the technical complexities of mining while you earn daily Bitcoin payouts proportional to your share of our total mining output. Returns are generated from block rewards and transaction fees.
What security measures are in place?
We implement institutional-grade security including multi-signature cold storage, biometric authentication, DDoS protection, and regular third-party audits. Our facilities have 24/7 physical security and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We've never experienced a security breach since our founding.
How often are payouts distributed?
Mining rewards are calculated daily and distributed every 24 hours. You can choose to automatically reinvest your earnings or withdraw them to your external wallet. Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours for standard requests or instantly for premium members.
What is the minimum investment?
Our starter plan begins at $500, making industrial-scale mining accessible to individual investors. Institutional investors can contact our team for custom plans starting at $250,000 with dedicated account management and enhanced features.
How can I verify the mining operations?
We provide transparent reporting with verifiable on-chain transactions. Investors can access real-time mining metrics, facility cameras (upon request), and third-party audit reports. Our public mining addresses are published quarterly for complete transparency.
