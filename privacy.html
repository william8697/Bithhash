Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

At Bithash, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information that applies to our Service, and your choices about the collection and use of your information. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Government-issued identification for KYC verification (e.g., passport, driver's license)
  • Proof of address documents (utility bills, bank statements)
  • Selfie or video verification for identity confirmation

1.2 Financial and Transaction Information

To process investments and withdrawals, we collect:

  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Transaction history and investment records
  • Bank account details (for fiat transactions where applicable)
  • Tax identification numbers where required by law

1.3 Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

  • IP address, browser type, and device characteristics
  • Operating system and system configuration
  • Pages visited, clickstream data, and session duration
  • Referring website addresses and exit pages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your account
  • Service Delivery: To process investments, withdrawals, and other transactions
  • Compliance: To verify identity and meet regulatory obligations (AML/KYC)
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activities
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and resolve technical issues
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and enhance platform functionality
  • Communications: To send important notices and service-related announcements

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

3.1 Storage Infrastructure

All user data is stored in secure databases with the following protections:

  • Data encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Network isolation through VPC peering
  • Regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing
  • Automated backups with point-in-time recovery

3.2 Authentication Security

We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure authentication:

  • Tokens signed with a strong secret key
  • Default token expiration set to 24 hours
  • Refresh token implementation for extended sessions
  • Multi-factor authentication available for all accounts

3.3 Additional Protections

  • All communications encrypted with TLS 1.2+
  • Regular security audits and code reviews
  • Strict access controls with role-based permissions
  • Dedicated security team monitoring 24/7

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in platform operations (payment processors, cloud hosting, KYC verification)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, subpoena, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Protection of Rights: To investigate potential violations or protect against harm

Note: We never sell your personal information to third-party marketers or advertisers.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality (session management, security)
  • Performance Cookies: Help analyze platform usage and performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences and settings
  • Security Cookies: Detect malicious activity and protect against CSRF attacks

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously granted permissions

To exercise these rights, please contact us at legal@bithash.com. We may require identity verification before processing requests.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 5 years after closure for compliance
  • Financial Records: Kept for 7 years to meet tax and regulatory requirements
  • KYC Documents: Stored for the duration of your account plus 5 years
  • Technical Logs: Maintained for 1 year for security monitoring

Data may be retained longer when required by law or for legitimate business needs.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

As a global platform, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required
  • Adequacy decisions for transfers to approved jurisdictions
  • Additional safeguards for sensitive data transfers

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users via email for material changes
  • Provide advance notice when required by law

Your continued use of our platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:

Email: legal@bithash.com

Address: 800 Plant St, Wilmington, DE 19801, United States

For account-specific inquiries, please contact us through your dashboard messaging system.
