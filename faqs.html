Account Questions

Why is KYC (Know Your Customer) required?

KYC verification is a standard security procedure required by financial regulations to prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities. At Bithash, we implement KYC to:

  • Protect our platform and users from fraudulent activities
  • Comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
  • Ensure the security of all transactions on our platform
  • Maintain the integrity of our investment ecosystem

KYC is typically required when your account reaches certain transaction thresholds or when you request withdrawals above specified limits.

What documents are accepted for KYC verification?

For KYC verification, we accept the following documents:

Identity Verification:

  • Passport (preferred)
  • Driver's license
  • National ID card

Address Verification:

  • Utility bill (electricity, water, gas) not older than 3 months
  • Bank statement
  • Government-issued tax bill

All documents must be clear, valid, and show your full name and residential address. Documents in languages other than English must be accompanied by a certified translation.

How long does KYC approval take?

Our standard KYC processing time is 1-3 business days after submission of all required documents. Processing may take longer during periods of high volume or if additional verification is required.

You can help expedite the process by:

  • Ensuring all documents are clear and legible
  • Providing complete documents (all corners visible, no cut-off information)
  • Making sure the name and address on all documents match exactly

You will receive email notifications about your KYC status throughout the verification process.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. Go to the Bithash login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if not received)
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

Security Tip: Always create a strong, unique password that you don't use for other services.

Transaction Questions

Fiat Currency Options:

How long do deposits and withdrawals take to process?

Processing times vary depending on the payment method and network conditions:

Deposit Processing Times:

  • Cryptocurrency: 5-30 minutes after required network confirmations
  • Bank Wire: 1-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: Instant to 1 hour
  • SEPA Transfer: 1-2 business days

Withdrawal Processing Times:

  • Cryptocurrency: Processed within 24 hours (usually faster)
  • Bank Wire: 2-5 business days
  • SEPA Transfer: 1-3 business days

All withdrawals are subject to security review, which may add additional processing time for large or unusual transactions. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the withdrawal process.

Can I withdraw using a different method than I deposited with?

Our withdrawal policy generally requires that funds be withdrawn using the same method they were deposited with, especially for fiat currency transactions. This is part of our anti-money laundering (AML) compliance measures.

Specific rules:

  • Cryptocurrency deposits: Can typically be withdrawn as cryptocurrency to any valid wallet address
  • Fiat currency deposits: Must be withdrawn back to the original funding source (same bank account or card)
  • Exceptions: May be considered on a case-by-case basis for verified accounts, subject to additional verification and potential fees

If you need to change your withdrawal method due to special circumstances, please contact our support team with details of your situation. We'll review your request and inform you of available options.

Are there any fees for deposits or withdrawals?

Deposit Fees:

  • Cryptocurrency: Free (network fees paid by sender)
  • Bank Wire: 0.5% (min $25, max $250)

  • SEPA Transfer: 0.1% (min €10, max €100)

Withdrawal Fees:

Security Questions

How do you secure my funds and data?

At Bithash, security is our top priority. We implement multiple layers of protection to safeguard your funds and personal information:

Fund Security:

  • 98% of digital assets held in cold storage (offline)
  • Multi-signature wallets for all hot wallets
  • Regular security audits by third-party firms
  • Insurance coverage for digital assets
  • Reserve fund to ensure liquidity

Data Protection:

  • Bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts
  • Regular penetration testing of our systems
  • Strict access controls and monitoring
  • GDPR-compliant data handling procedures

Account Security Features:

  • Login notifications
  • Withdrawal confirmation emails
  • Device management and recognition
  • Session timeouts
  • IP address monitoring

We continuously update our security measures to address emerging threats in the cryptocurrency space.

Do you store my private keys or wallet credentials?

No, Bithash does not have access to your personal wallet private keys. Our security model is designed so that we never have access to your private keys under any circumstances.

For funds held in our platform:

  • We use institutional-grade custody solutions
  • Private keys are secured using hardware security modules (HSMs)
  • Keys are geographically distributed and require multiple approvals for access

When you withdraw to an external wallet:

  • You are responsible for securing your own private keys
  • We strongly recommend using hardware wallets for significant amounts
  • Always keep backup copies of your recovery phrases in secure locations

Remember: If you lose access to your private keys or recovery phrases, no one - including Bithash - can recover your funds. Always practice good key management hygiene.

What security measures should I take for my account?

While we implement robust security measures on our end, you play a crucial role in keeping your account secure. Here are our top recommendations:

Essential Security Practices:

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) rather than SMS when possible
  • Use a Strong, Unique Password: At least 12 characters with mixed cases, numbers, and symbols
  • Beware of Phishing: Always check URLs before entering credentials; we'll never ask for your password via email
  • Secure Your Email: Use 2FA on your email account as it's often the weakest link
  • Monitor Account Activity: Regularly review login history and withdrawal activity

Advanced Security Measures:

  • Use a dedicated device for cryptocurrency transactions
  • Consider using a password manager
  • Whitelist withdrawal addresses where possible
  • Set up withdrawal limits and notifications
  • Keep your devices and browsers updated

If you suspect any unauthorized access to your account, immediately change your password, revoke any active sessions through your account settings, and contact our security team.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized account access?

If you suspect unauthorized access to your Bithash account, take these immediate steps:

  1. Change Your Password: Log in (if possible) and change your password immediately
  2. Enable 2FA: If not already enabled, set up two-factor authentication
  3. Revoke Active Sessions: Go to Account Settings > Security and terminate all active sessions
  4. Check Recent Activity: Review login history and withdrawal activity for suspicious actions
  5. Contact Support: Immediately notify our security team at security@bithash.com
  6. Freeze Withdrawals: If available in your account settings, temporarily disable withdrawals

After taking these steps:

  • Monitor your email for any unauthorized password reset requests
  • Check other online accounts that may use the same credentials
  • Consider scanning your devices for malware
  • Update security questions if applicable

Our security team will work with you to investigate any suspicious activity and help secure your account. Response times are typically under 1 hour for urgent security matters.

Referral Program

How does the referral system work?

Bithash's referral program rewards you for introducing new users to our platform. Here's how it works:

  1. Share your unique referral link with friends, family, or your network
  2. When someone signs up using your link and makes their first deposit, they become your referral
  3. You earn a percentage of their investment returns for the duration of their investment
  4. Referral bonuses are credited to your account daily

Current Referral Tiers:

  • Standard: 5% of referral's earnings (1-5 active referrals)
  • Silver: 7% of referral's earnings (6-20 active referrals)
  • Gold: 10% of referral's earnings (21-50 active referrals)
  • Platinum: 15% of referral's earnings (51+ active referrals)

Referral bonuses are paid in the same cryptocurrency as the original investment. There are no limits to how much you can earn through referrals.

Where can I find my referral link?

Your personal referral link is available in your account dashboard. To access it:

  1. Log in to your Bithash account
  2. Navigate to the "Referrals" section in your dashboard
  3. Copy your unique referral link from the designated area

You can also find:

  • Referral tracking statistics (clicks, signups, active referrals)
  • Customizable referral banners and graphics
  • Share buttons for social media and email
  • Your current referral tier and progress to the next level

For marketing professionals or those with large networks, we offer custom referral solutions and dedicated support. Contact our partnerships team at affiliates@bithash.com for more information.

What bonuses do I earn from referrals?

Referral bonuses are based on your current tier and your referrals' investment activity:

Standard Bonuses:

Additional Tier Benefits:

Are there any rules or restrictions for referrals?

To maintain fairness and prevent abuse, our referral program has the following rules:

General Rules:

Marketing Restrictions:

  • No spamming (email, social media, forums, etc.)
  • No misleading claims about potential earnings
  • No use of Bithash trademarks without permission

Fraud Prevention:

  • We monitor for fraudulent activity including but not limited to:
