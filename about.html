Our Mission

Verifiable Hashpower, Bitcoin-Backed Loans, Seamless Trading

BitHash was founded in 2021 — a year defined by Bitcoin's all‑time high of $69,000, a historic China mining ban, and the first major wave of institutional adoption. For most retail investors, crypto mining had become inaccessible. ASIC miners were sold out for months. Hosting fees tripled overnight. The cloud mining space was riddled with Ponzi schemes.

We saw a different path. BitHash was built to deliver verifiable hashpower, Bitcoin‑backed loans up to 3x, and seamless spot trading in a single transparent ecosystem. Today, over 3 million users trust BitHash across more than 120 countries.

Digital Gold Thesis

Bitcoin Is Digital Gold. Mining Is Your Pickaxe.
