From Genesis Block to Cloud Mining Innovation

Bithash was founded in 2021, a year defined by Bitcoin's all time high of $69,000, a historic China mining ban, and the first major wave of institutional adoption. For most retail investors, crypto mining had become inaccessible. ASIC miners were sold out for months. Hosting fees tripled overnight. We saw a different path. Bithash was built to bridge proof-of-work fundamentals with decentralized finance accessibility. Our mission is simple: deliver verifiable hashpower, Bitcoin-backed loans, and seamless spot trading in a single transparent ecosystem. No hype. Just real infrastructure powering real yield. Today, over 3 million users trust Bithash with their mining, lending, and trading needs across more than 120 countries.

Bitcoin Is Digital Gold: Why Mining Remains the Smartest Long Term Play

Bitcoin has earned its reputation as digital gold. Its fixed supply of 21 million coins makes it the hardest asset ever created. At Bithash, mining Bitcoin is the closest thing to mining digital gold in the 21st century. Our cloud mining contracts allow you to acquire Bitcoin without purchasing hardware, managing heat, or negotiating energy contracts. The long-term trajectory of Bitcoin as digital gold remains intact despite market volatility. Each halving reduces the flow of new Bitcoin, making existing holdings more valuable over time. We have helped over 3 million users accumulate more than 2,800 Bitcoin since launch — that's 2,800 digital gold coins mined, held, and borrowed against without ever touching a physical ASIC miner.

Bitcoin Backed Loans: Unlock Up to 3x Your Holdings

One of the most powerful innovations in modern crypto finance is the ability to use mined assets as collateral without triggering a taxable event. Bithash offers Bitcoin-backed loans up to three times your total holdings, depending on your mining history and collateral quality. You deposit Bitcoin into our multi-signature cold storage vault, and we lend you stablecoins at a loan-to-value ratio that can reach 300% of your deposited collateral. That means if you hold 1 Bitcoin, you can borrow up to 3 Bitcoin worth of stablecoins. Interest rates start at 6% APR, and your mining rewards continue accumulating daily. Unlike DeFi protocols that limit you to 50-60% LTV, Bithash offers a high-leverage hybrid model with manual collateral monitoring. Our 3x loan product launched in early 2026 and has already funded over $15 million in stablecoin loans — with zero unwanted liquidations.

Seamless Spot Trading, DEX Aggregation & Cross Chain Bridges

A mining account shouldn't force you to withdraw to an external exchange. Bithash solves this with an embedded trading engine supporting spot trading for over 50 cryptocurrencies. Mine Bitcoin, bridge rewards to Ethereum for DeFi yield, and borrow against that position — all within Bithash.
