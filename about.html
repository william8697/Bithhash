From Genesis Block to Cloud Mining Innovation

Bithash was founded in 2021, a year defined by Bitcoin's all time high of 69,000 dollars, a historic China mining ban, and the first major wave of institutional adoption. For most retail investors, crypto mining had become inaccessible. ASIC miners were sold out for months. Hosting fees tripled overnight. And the cloud mining space was riddled with Ponzi schemes promising impossible returns. We saw a different path. Bithash was built to bridge the gap between proof of work fundamentals and decentralized finance accessibility. Our mission is simple: deliver verifiable hashpower, Bitcoin backed loans, and seamless spot trading in a single transparent ecosystem. No hype. No hidden terms. Just real infrastructure powering real yield. Whether you are mining Bitcoin, using liquid staking derivatives as collateral, or executing cross chain swaps, Bithash is designed to keep you in control while maximizing capital efficiency. We are not just another cloud mining platform. We are a full stack crypto financial service built by engineers who survived the 2021 volatility and emerged stronger. Today, over 3 million users trust Bithash with their mining, lending, and trading needs across more than 120 countries.

Proof of Work at Scale

Proof of work remains the most battle tested consensus mechanism in crypto. At Bithash, we honor that by owning every piece of hardware we operate. Our mining farms span three continents, including North America, South America, and Northern Europe, running over 12,000 next generation ASIC miners. Our fleet includes the Bitmain Antminer S21 Pro, MicroBT Whatsminer M66S, and Canaan Avalon A1566. Total hashrate exceeds 450 petahashes per second, with a fleet efficiency averaging 22 joules per terahash. We secure long term power purchase agreements at 0.035 US dollars per kilowatt hour in Paraguay using hydroelectric power, 0.042 US dollars per kilowatt hour in Texas using flare gas mitigation, and 0.038 US dollars per kilowatt hour in Norway using stranded hydro power. These energy contracts are fixed for five years, insulating our users from energy price volatility. When you buy a cloud mining contract on Bithash, you receive daily payouts based on actual block rewards minus disclosed operational costs. Every payout is verifiable through our block explorer integration. We call this radical transparency, a direct response to the opaque cloud mining models that hurt retail users in past market cycles.

Bitcoin Is Digital Gold

Bitcoin has earned its reputation as digital gold for good reason. Like physical gold, Bitcoin is scarce, divisible, and independent of central bank monetary policy. But unlike gold, Bitcoin is verifiable, portable, and programmable. Its fixed supply of 21 million coins makes it the hardest asset ever created. At Bithash, we believe that mining Bitcoin is the closest thing to mining digital gold in the 21st century. When you mine with us, you are not speculating on short term price movements. You are accumulating a provably scarce asset at a production cost, similar to how gold miners operate in the physical world. Our cloud mining contracts allow you to acquire Bitcoin without purchasing hardware, managing heat, or negotiating energy contracts. You simply buy hashpower, and we deliver daily Bitcoin payouts directly to your wallet. The long term trajectory of Bitcoin as digital gold remains intact despite market volatility. Each halving reduces the flow of new Bitcoin, making existing holdings more valuable over time.

Whether you are a long term holder, a dollar cost averaging investor, or someone looking for passive income, mining Bitcoin through Bithash offers a disciplined way to grow your digital gold reserves. We have helped over 3 million users accumulate more than 2,800 Bitcoin since our launch. That is 2,800 digital gold coins mined, held, and borrowed against without ever touching a physical ASIC miner. The digital gold thesis is not marketing. It is mathematics. And Bithash is your most efficient pickaxe.

Bitcoin Backed Loans: Up to 3x Your Holdings

One of the most powerful innovations in modern crypto finance is the ability to use mined assets as collateral without triggering a taxable event. Bithash takes this further than any competitor. We offer Bitcoin backed loans up to three times your total holdings, depending on your mining history and collateral quality. Here is how it works. You deposit Bitcoin or wrapped Bitcoin into our multi signature cold storage vault. We then lend you stablecoins such as USDT, USDC, or DAI at a loan to value ratio that can reach 300 percent of your deposited collateral value. That means if you hold 1 Bitcoin, you can borrow up to 3 Bitcoin worth of stablecoins. Interest rates start at 6 percent annual percentage rate, and your mining rewards continue accumulating daily. You can even use those rewards to pay down interest or principal automatically.

Unlike DeFi protocols like Aave or Compound, which typically limit you to 50 or 60 percent loan to value and expose you to liquidation risk during flash crashes, Bithash offers a high leverage hybrid model. We manually monitor collateral health and offer margin call warnings 48 hours before any action is taken. This is not algorithmic lending. It is relationship backed lending powered by real hashproof verification and your active mining position. The 3x leverage is reserved for verified users with consistent mining activity, but even new users can access up to 2x leverage immediately after KYC. With over 3 million users in our ecosystem, our loan product has become one of the most trusted in the industry.

For users who also stake their mining rewards into our liquidity pools, we offer yield farming opportunities that can offset loan interest entirely. The result is powerful. You never sell your Bitcoin at the wrong time. You borrow up to three times against it, earn mining yield simultaneously, and wait for your target exit price. We have processed over 1,200 Bitcoin backed loans since 2022 with zero liquidations that were not voluntarily accepted by the borrower. Our 3x loan product launched in early 2026 and has already funded over 15 million dollars in stablecoin loans. That track record matters in an industry where over lending has destroyed portfolios. Our loan desk operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, with approvals typically completed within four hours of collateral confirmation.

Seamless Spot Trading & DEX Aggregation

A mining account should not force you to withdraw to an external centralized exchange like Binance or Coinbase just to trade. That adds network fees, transaction delays, and counterparty risk. Bithash solves this with an embedded trading engine that supports spot trading for over 50 cryptocurrencies. Our supported assets include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Cardano, Dogecoin, and major stablecoins. But we went further. Our platform includes a DEX aggregator that sources liquidity from Uniswap, PancakeSwap, and Curve, giving you competitive spreads without leaving your dashboard. For users moving assets across different blockchain networks, we have integrated cross chain bridges supporting Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and BNB Chain. This means you can mine Bitcoin on our proof of work network, bridge your rewards to Ethereum for DeFi yield, and then borrow against that position, all within Bithash.

We have effectively built a non custodial layer on top of a custodial mining operation. This rare hybrid gives you security for your long term holdings and flexibility for your active capital. Our trading engine executes orders in under 500 milliseconds with zero downtime since our mainnet launch in 2022. You can also set recurring buys using your mining rewards, a feature popular among dollar cost averaging investors. The integration between mining, lending, and trading is seamless because we built all three systems from scratch rather than patching together third party APIs.

Halving Cycle Strategy & Smart Contracts

Every serious crypto investor understands Bitcoin's four year halving cycle. The next halving is projected for April 2028, when block rewards will drop from 3.125 Bitcoin to 1.5625 Bitcoin. Smart mining strategies require positioning before the difficulty adjustment. Bithash allows you to lock in hashpower contracts for 12, 24, or 36 months, effectively hedged against rising difficulty. Our smart contract architecture automatically recalculates your daily payout based on real time network hashrate and block reward, then disburses funds to your wallet with gas optimization. We batch thousands of transactions to keep your withdrawal fees near zero. We also integrate with layer 2 solutions like the Lightning Network for instant, low cost Bitcoin transfers.

For users who want to compound earnings, our auto compounding feature reinvests mining rewards into additional hashpower, creating an exponential growth curve without manual intervention. This is not theoretical. Over 500,000 of our 3 million users currently run auto compounding strategies on Bithash, with average annualized yields between 18 percent and 35 percent depending on Bitcoin's price and network difficulty. We also provide a halving calculator that projects your earnings under different difficulty scenarios.

Transparency, Compliance & Community

The crypto industry has a memory problem. Projects appear in bull markets and vanish in bear markets. Bithash was born in the volatility of 2021 and has survived two major drawdowns, including the 2022 FTX collapse and the 2024 post halving shakeout. Our survival comes down to three structural advantages. First, proof of reserves. Every Bitcoin held in our loan collateral vaults and mining reserves is published monthly via a Merkle tree audit from a third party firm. Second, regulatory compliance. We are registered as a money services business in the United States and Canada, with full know your customer and anti money laundering protocols. Third, community governance. Bithash token holders, known as BTH, vote on key protocol parameters including maintenance fee caps, new asset listings for our trading engine, and loan interest rate floors.

This is not a decentralized autonomous organization gimmick. It is a functional governance model where long term miners have actual influence. We do not promise moonshots. We promise uptime, transparency, and a sustainable yield model that works in bear markets and bull markets alike. Our team publishes a monthly operations report that includes energy costs, hardware failure rates, and pool performance metrics.
