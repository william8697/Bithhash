And the cloud mining space was riddled with Ponzi schemes promising impossible returns. We saw a different path.

Bithash was built to bridge the gap between proof of work fundamentals and decentralized finance accessibility. Just real infrastructure powering real yield.

Whether you are mining Bitcoin, using liquid staking derivatives as collateral, or executing cross chain swaps, Bithash is designed to keep you in control while maximizing capital efficiency. Proof of Work at Scale: Multi-Continent Mining Infrastructure

Proof of work remains the most battle tested consensus mechanism in crypto. At Bithash, we honor that by owning every piece of hardware we operate. Our mining farms span three continents, including North America, South America, and Northern Europe, running over 12,000 next generation ASIC miners. Our fleet includes the Bitmain Antminer S21 Pro, MicroBT Whatsminer M66S, and Canaan Avalon A1566.

Total hashrate exceeds 450 petahashes per second, with a fleet efficiency averaging 22 joules per terahash. We secure long term power purchase agreements at 0.035 USD/kWh in Paraguay (hydroelectric), 0.042 USD/kWh in Texas (flare gas mitigation), and 0.038 USD/kWh in Norway (stranded hydro power).

When you buy a cloud mining contract on Bithash, you receive daily payouts based on actual block rewards minus disclosed operational costs. Every payout is verifiable through our block explorer integration. With over 3 million users and growing, our infrastructure has proven itself across multiple market cycles.

Bitcoin Is Digital Gold: Why Mining Remains the Smartest Long Term Play

Bitcoin has earned its reputation as digital gold for good reason. Like physical gold, Bitcoin is scarce, divisible, and independent of central bank monetary policy. But unlike gold, Bitcoin is verifiable, portable, and programmable. Its fixed supply of 21 million coins makes it the hardest asset ever created.

At Bithash, we believe that mining Bitcoin is the closest thing to mining digital gold in the 21st century. When you mine with us, you are not speculating on short term price movements. You are accumulating a provably scarce asset at a production cost, similar to how gold miners operate in the physical world.

Our cloud mining contracts allow you to acquire Bitcoin without purchasing hardware, managing heat, or negotiating energy contracts. You simply buy hashpower, and we deliver daily Bitcoin payouts directly to your wallet. We have helped over 3 million users accumulate more than 2,800 Bitcoin since our launch. The digital gold thesis is not marketing. It is mathematics. And Bithash is your most efficient pickaxe.
