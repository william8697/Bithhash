Withdrawal Instructions

Bitcoin Withdrawal Guide

  1. Specify the USD amount you wish to withdraw
  2. Enter your Bitcoin wallet address (SegWit/Bech32 recommended)
  3. Review the network fee and confirm the transaction
  4. Wait for processing (typically within 24 hours)
  5. Funds will be sent to your wallet after 1-3 confirmations
Minimum Withdrawal

The minimum withdrawal is $10 USD equivalent. Smaller amounts will not be processed.

Exchange Rate

The exchange rate is locked when you submit your withdrawal request and is valid for 15 minutes.

Bank Transfer Guide

  1. Enter the amount you want to withdraw (minimum $100 required)
  2. Provide accurate bank account information
  3. Review the wire fee and confirm the transaction
  4. Wait for processing (3-5 business days)
  5. Funds will be credited to your bank account
Processing Fees

Bank withdrawals incur a $25 wire fee.

Security Verification

For security, your first bank withdrawal may require additional verification.
