Deposit Instructions

Bitcoin Deposit Guide

  1. Specify the USD amount you intend to deposit
  2. Transfer the exact BTC amount provided to the designated wallet address
  3. Wait for 1–3 blockchain confirmations (typically within 10–30 minutes)
  4. Upon confirmation, the funds will be automatically credited to your account

Credit Card Deposit Guide
