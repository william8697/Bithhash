Deposit Instructions

Bitcoin Deposit Guide

  1. Specify the USD amount you intend to deposit
  2. Transfer the exact amount to the designated wallet address
  3. Wait for 1–3 blockchain confirmations (typically within 10–30 minutes)
  4. Upon confirmation, the funds will be automatically credited to your account
Minimum Deposit

The minimum deposit is $10 USD equivalent. Smaller amounts will not be credited.

Exchange Rate

The exchange rate is locked when you generate the deposit address and is valid for 15 minutes.

Credit Card Deposit Guide

  1. Enter the amount you want to deposit (minimum $10 required)
  2. Provide accurate card and billing information
  3. Complete 3D Secure authentication if prompted by your bank
  4. Once approved, funds will be credited to your account instantly
Processing Fees

Credit card deposits incur a 3.5% processing fee.

Security Verification

For security, your first deposit may require additional verification documents.
