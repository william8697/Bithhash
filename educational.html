Real Earning Example

Let's say you get 500 TH/s (terahashes per second) of mining power. This means your rented computers can make 500 trillion calculations per second.

Your 500 TH/s joins a mining pool with 50,000 TH/s total power.

Your share: 500 ÷ 50,000 = 1% of the pool

When the pool successfully mines a block (earning 3.125 BTC), you get 1% of that reward.

Your cut: 1% of 3.125 BTC = 0.03125 BTC

This gets distributed daily along with transaction fee rewards. As Bitcoin's price changes, so does the dollar value of your earnings. The best cloud mining contracts to buy 2026 often offer competitive terms that align with this payout structure.

Bitcoin Mining Terms You Should Know

Hashrate

Mining power measurement. 1 TH/s = 1 trillion calculations per second. Higher hashrate = more earning potential.

Mining Pool

Group of miners combining power. Pools earn rewards more often, then split them fairly among members.

Block Reward

New Bitcoin created for miners. Currently 3.125 BTC per block. Halves every 4 years.

Difficulty

How hard it is to mine Bitcoin. Adjusts every 2 weeks based on total network mining power.

Cloud Contract

Agreement to rent mining power for a specific period, usually 12-36 months.

ASIC Miner

Specialized Bitcoin mining machine. Much more efficient than regular computers.
